Integration Bee

Physics and Astronomy Club - IIT Roorkee

About the Event

The Integration Bee is a high-energy competition where participants solve complex integrals within a given time frame. It is designed to test mathematical intuition, speed, and accuracy.

Format:

  • Participants compete in multiple rounds, solving integrals of increasing difficulty.
  • The first round is a written test; the top performers advance to the knockout stage.
  • In the final round, competitors solve problems on a whiteboard in a time-limited setting.
