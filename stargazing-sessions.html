Stargazing Sessions

Physics and Astronomy Club - IIT Roorkee

About the Event

PaAC hosts night sky observation sessions using telescopes, where participants can observe planets, star clusters, and deep-sky objects.

Typical Observation List:

  • Moon – Craters and mountain ranges (with telescope filters).
  • Jupiter & Saturn – Cloud bands, Great Red Spot, and rings.
  • Orion Nebula (M42) – Star formation region.
  • Andromeda Galaxy (M31) – Closest spiral galaxy.
  • Perseid Meteor Shower (Annual Event – August).
