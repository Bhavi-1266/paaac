Journal Camp

Physics and Astronomy Club - IIT Roorkee

About the Event

Journal Camp is an initiative by PaAC where students come together to read, present, and discuss research papers in physics, mathematics, and related fields. The aim is to foster a culture of academic curiosity and collaborative learning.

Event Details:

  • Frequency: Sessions are held periodically throughout the academic year.
  • Format: Each session focuses on a specific paper or topic. Participants are encouraged to present their insights, followed by group discussions.
  • Topics: Previous sessions have covered areas such as quantum mechanics, astrophysics, and advanced mathematical theories.
  • Eligibility: Open to all students with an interest in physics and mathematics.
  • Registration: Announcements are made prior to each session with details on how to participate.
