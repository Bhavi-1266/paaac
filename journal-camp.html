Journal Camp

Physics and Astronomy Club - IIT Roorkee

About the Event

PaAC’s Journal Camp is a collaborative learning program where participants read, present, and discuss research papers on mathematics and physics.

Structure:

  • Weekly sessions covering topics in pure and applied mathematics.
  • Participants are assigned papers, followed by discussion sessions.
  • Expert faculty occasionally join to provide insights.

Topics Covered (Past Sessions):

  • "Fourier Transform and Signal Processing" – Prof. Ramesh Kumar
  • "Mathematical Foundations of Machine Learning" – Dr. Ananya Sharma
  • "Number Theory and Cryptography" – Student-led discussion
